Princeton, NJ, is not just home to the prestigious university and charming streets; it also boasts a vibrant food scene that won't break the bank. From classic sandwich shops to cozy cafes, this town is filled with delicious options that cater to every palate. Whether you're a local or just passing through, you'll find plenty of spots that serve up satisfying meals without the hefty price tag.

As you explore the culinary landscape of Princeton, you'll discover a delightful mix of well-loved institutions and hidden gems. Picture yourself savoring a foot-long hoagie from Hoagie Haven, or grabbing a quick Mediterranean bite at Olives. With so many affordable eateries to choose from, the Wanderlog team is excited to share our favorite cheap eats that promise to fill your belly and warm your heart. So, grab your appetite and get ready to explore the best budget-friendly dining options this charming town has to offer!
